The Problem

Africa's mineral trade faces significant challenges that hinder growth and trust:

  • Fragmented mineral trade - No centralized marketplace
  • High fraud & counterparty risk - Lack of verification systems
  • No price discovery - Limited market transparency
  • Slow deal execution - Manual processes and paperwork

Our Solution

A buyer-centric digital marketplace that provides:

Verified Suppliers

All suppliers undergo rigorous verification and due diligence processes.

Escrow & Block Fund Logic

Secure payment structures protect both buyers and sellers throughout transactions.

Structured Bidding

Transparent bidding process ensures fair market price discovery.

About MTA

Mineral Trading Africa - Africa's Trusted Mineral Marketplace

Our Mission

Mineral Trading Africa (MTA) is designed to become Africa's trusted mineral marketplace, connecting verified buyers with reliable suppliers across the continent.
